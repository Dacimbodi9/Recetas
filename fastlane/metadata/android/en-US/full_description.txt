Recetas is a free, beautifully designed app to manage your recipes with privacy in mind.

✨ Features

*  **Offline & Private:** All your data stays on your device. No tracking, no servers.
*  **Smart Search:** Fast fuzzy search by name, or select ingredients you have in your fridge to find matching recipes.
*  **Dietary Compliance:** Permanent dietary filters (Vegan, Gluten-Free, Keto, etc.) with incompatible recipe warnings.
*  **Organization:** Create folders, rate your recipes, and manage your favorites.
*  **Premium Experience:** Beautiful dark mode aesthetics and highly customizable settings.

Discover over 1,000 built-in recipes, create your own, and fully control your data.
